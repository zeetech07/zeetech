What is Data Analysis?

Data Analysis involves the process of inspecting, cleaning, and modeling data to discover useful information and inform decision-making. Data Analysts utilize statistics, data visualization, and data manipulation techniques to uncover trends and insights.

Why Learn Data Analysis?

  • High demand for Data Analysts across industries
  • Competitive salary packages
  • Opportunities to work in diverse sectors
  • Ability to make impactful data-driven decisions
