What is Stock Market?

The stock market is where investors buy and sell shares of publicly traded companies. This market is a vital part of the economy, offering individuals and institutions a place to invest and grow wealth through buying and selling securities.

Why Learn Stock Market Trading?

  • High potential for financial growth
  • Opportunities for both short-term and long-term investments
  • Gain an understanding of global financial markets
  • Become proficient in technical and fundamental analysis
  • Work independently as a trader or in finance-related roles
