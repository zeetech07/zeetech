What is Digital Marketing?

Digital Marketing refers to the use of digital channels and tools to promote products and services. It includes various tactics like SEO, social media marketing, email marketing, and online advertising to reach target audiences effectively.

Why Learn Digital Marketing?

  • Work with diverse brands and industries
