What is Software Testing?

Software Testing is the process of evaluating a software application to detect any differences between existing and required conditions, often focusing on bugs or performance issues. It plays a critical role in ensuring software quality.

Why Learn Software Testing?

  • High demand in the tech industry
  • Opportunities to work in multiple domains
  • Ensures software meets high-quality standards
  • Improves career prospects with testing expertise
